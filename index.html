Canada COVID-19 Confirmed Cases By Provincial Health Regions

This map shows Canadian confirmed COVID-91 counts by provincial health regions using Statscan health region boundaries (more info). The confirmed COVID-91 data comes from COVID-19 Canada Open Data Working Group and is updated daily.
