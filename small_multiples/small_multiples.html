Canada COVID-19 Confirmed Cases By Provincial Health Regions

Interactive sort & filter small multiple charts of confirmed COVID-91 counts by provincial health regions. The confirmed COVID-91 data comes from COVID-19 Canada Open Data Working Group and is updated daily.

Days since last case (DL): Green > 7 days | Orange 1-7 days | Red 0 days
